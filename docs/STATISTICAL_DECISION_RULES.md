# Statistical decision rules

- Endpoint: advanced fibrosis defined as histologic fibrosis stage ≥F3 vs ≤F2 where staging is available.
- Effect sizes: within-cohort logistic regression odds ratio (OR) per 1-SD endotype score.
- Synthesis: DerSimonian–Laird random-effects meta-analysis of cohort log-ORs; heterogeneity reported as I².
- Transportability: leave-one-cohort-out (LOCO) evaluation; discrimination reported as AUC; calibration reported with calibration slope and binned reliability; net benefit summarized with decision-curve analysis.
- Robustness: sensitivity analysis over a small grid of NMF ranks and random seeds (reported descriptively; not used for post-hoc selection).
